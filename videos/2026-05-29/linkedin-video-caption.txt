Patch windows often expose a visibility problem before they expose a tooling problem.

BayouOps Suite Pro is a local-first operational readiness and visibility layer for teams that need clearer patch, compliance, stale-system, and SSL/certificate reporting before CAB review or management escalation.

It is built to help admins and IT managers answer the question leadership eventually asks:

"Are we exposed?"

The goal is practical visibility:
- patch readiness context
- stale and unsupported system review
- SSL/certificate exposure awareness
- exportable evidence for CAB, SOX, and audit conversations
- executive-ready operational summaries

This is not positioned as a replacement for existing patching, endpoint, or RMM platforms. It is a reporting and readiness layer that helps teams make better use of the evidence they already collect.

GitHub demo materials and walkthrough content are included with the project.
