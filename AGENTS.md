# Core Product Direction

BayouOps Suite Pro is an operational visibility and advisory platform.

The platform is intentionally designed to:
- improve operational awareness
- reduce visibility gaps
- support safer maintenance coordination
- provide readiness insight
- generate executive-friendly exports
- assist operators without replacing them

---

# Architectural Guardrails

## DO

- Build read-only visibility tooling
- Improve operational awareness
- Support export/report workflows
- Provide explainable recommendations
- Preserve operator control
- Keep tooling lightweight
- Focus on operational clarity
- Support small and medium IT teams

## DO NOT

- Implement autonomous remediation
- Implement unattended patching
- Implement remote execution
- Build RMM-style control systems
- Replace operator approval workflows
- Introduce unnecessary platform bloat

---

# Operational Philosophy

The platform should help operators answer questions like:

- What is risky?
- What is stale?
- What needs attention?
- What should be reviewed first?
- What operational coordination risks exist?
- What should leadership know?

The platform should NOT automatically take destructive action.
