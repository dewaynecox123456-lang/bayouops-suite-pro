# About BayouOps Suite Pro

BayouOps Suite Pro is a lightweight, local-first operational visibility and
reporting toolkit for IT operators, consultants, small teams, and customer
environments that need clear readiness evidence without heavy infrastructure.

It helps organize local software, patch, readiness, and executive reporting
signals into customer-readable exports and handoff materials.

## Operating Model

BayouOps is designed to stay transparent and operator-controlled:

- read-only visibility and reporting
- no endpoint modifications
- no telemetry
- no background services
- no hidden polling or startup persistence
- local files and local exports

Operators choose when to generate demo data, render dashboards, create export
packs, run collectors, or open reports.
