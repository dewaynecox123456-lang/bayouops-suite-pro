BAYOUOPS QUICK START
=================================

1. Extract the ZIP file first.
2. Open the BayouOps folder.
3. Double-click BayouOps.exe
4. Click "Load Demo Environment"
5. Review dashboards and reports.

Exports are saved under:
./exports/

Logs are saved under:
./logs/

BayouOps is read-only software and does not modify endpoints.
